Green Pesto with garlic

This recipe includes very few ingredients like basil, garlic, pines and olive oil.

Its so simple yet so delicious!

Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Pines
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Crush the Basil and the pines together in a mortar
  2. Add the garlic and go on crushing it
  3. Add the olive oil and mix it up
  4. That's it!
